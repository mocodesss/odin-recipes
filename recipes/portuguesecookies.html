Estela's "Rosquilla" Cookies

Description

A citrusy, crispy shortbread like cookie that will transcend you to you roommate's Portuguese grandmother's kitchen table for coffee and a chat about how beautiful you are. Why just have one when you can have 12? If you are wondering,"rosquilla" means donut.

This batch will make you enough cookies to send a box to each of your grandchildren. Oh, and these cookie travel very well!

Ingredients

  • 6 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cups of sugar
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 1/2 a cup of (a little less) Mazola oil
  • 1 box of lemon instant pudding

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix everything together very well.
  3. Mend dough into the shape of the "rosquilla" and set on cookie sheet.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, turn the tray around, and bake for 15 more minutes (total baking time:30 minutes).
  5. Let cool on baking rack and enjoy!
