Michele D's Scones

Description

Honestly, get ready for the best chocolate chip scones you've ever eaten. They taste doughy but in the best way. You cannot say "no" to these, no matter the discipline you have around food. Consider yourself warned! Enjoy at any time of the day. Will likely be gone within their first 24 hours on this earth.

Total prep time: I've seen Michelle D do it in about 8 minutes, but it will likely take you about 20-25 minutes. If you are a novice, it will take you longer, but that's on you. Total baking time: 12-14 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1/2 a cup of sugar (+ a little extra for sprinkling)
  • 1/4 of a cup (1/2 a stick) of unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • Chocolate chips (however much you like)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Stir all dry ingredients together until well combined
  3. Mix (with mixer) all dry ingredients well
  4. Add softened butter (with clean hands)
  5. Blend together butter and all dry ingredients (still may be a little chunky)
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips
  7. Add the buttermilk (with mixer)
  8. Knead the dough into a ball and cut into 4 quarters; make each quarter into a ball
  9. Coat (rub) each ball with buttermilk and sprinkle some sugar on top
  10. Cut each ball into halves and then into thirds
  11. Bake for 6-7 mins, switch rack placement, and bake again for 6-7 minutes (total baking time: 12-14 minutes)
